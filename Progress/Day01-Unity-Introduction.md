## 📚 Topics Covered

### Core Components of Every Game

1. **Game Engine**
2. **Assets**
   - Character
   - Tree
   - House
   - Music
   - Footsteps
   - Gun Sound
3. **Scripts**
   - Example: If the player presses **Space**, the player jumps.
4. **Physics**
   - Gravity
   - Collision
   - Falling
5. **Animation**
   - Walking
   - Standing
   - Jumping
6. **Audio**
   - Voice
   - Music
   - Footsteps
7. **UI (User Interface)**
   - Health Bar
   - Mini Map
   - Coins

## 👨‍💻 Types of Game Developers

### Gameplay Programmer
Develops game logic such as:
- Running
- Jumping
- Quests

### AI Programmer
Creates enemy and NPC behavior.

### Graphics Programmer
Works on:
- Shadows
- Water
- Lighting
- Shaders

> Requires strong mathematics knowledge.

### Physics Programmer
Develops systems like:
- Gravity
- Collision
- Cloth Simulation
- Vehicle Physics
- Destruction

### Network Programmer
Creates multiplayer systems.

### Technical Artist
Connects artists with programmers and helps integrate art into the game.

## 🎯 Key Takeaways

- Every game is made up of multiple systems working together.
- A game engine combines graphics, physics, audio, scripting, animation, and UI into one development environment.
- Different programmers specialize in different areas of game development.
